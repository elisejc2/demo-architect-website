Who We Are

Sponseller Design Group was founded in spring of 2024 by principals Robert M Sponseller FAIA, Jennifer Chen AIA, and Huan Chou Chang AIA. Three colleagues that had been working together for over 15 years in a previous office in Washington DC.

Robert Sponseller FAIA was a principal leading design on some of the most significant sites in Washington DC and surround areas. Huan Chou Change is an experienced project manager with expertise on all phases of design and construction. Jennifer Chen is a design team leader guiding the development and realization of numerous complex multip building projects in the region.
